Example 1



We have some line segments on vector layer with linestring geometry as in figure 1. We want to get two more line segments as in figure 2. AC segment with entered length 60.425 and BC segment with entered length 120.82. Both segments have a common endpoint C.




Fig. 1

Fig. 2



Snapping is enabled.




Advanced Digitizing panel from View, Panels menu is visible.




Left click on the plugin icon to start.



Click Enable advanced digitizing tool button.



Move the cursor to snap point A and left click.



Press d to move cursor to d field in the Advanced Digitizing panel. Type 60,425 and press Enter to lock the distance.





Right click to accept the distance from point A. The color of the circle should become slightly darker.




Move the mouse cursor to point B to snap to point B and left-click.




Press d to move cursor to d field in the Advanced Digitizing panel. Type 120,82 and press Enter to lock the distance.






Right click to accept the distance from point B. You go to the intersection selection. Now the Advanced digitizing panel and snapping are temporarily disabled by the plugin.




Move the cursor slightly towards the top of the screen to mark the upper intersection with red segments as shown.




Left click to select. The result as in the picture.




Now you should check the obtained distances, because calculations are performed on floating point numbers with some precision. Click the Measure Line button.




Click Cartesian.




Click on points A, C and B in turn.




The measured distances in this example are: 60.425000±0.000001 and 120.820000±0.000001
