Example 2



We have two line segments with entered lengths of 68 and 37 and two points A and B as in Figure 1. We want to determine the intersection of the distances of the given segments from points A and B respectively without entering the distance from the keyboard. The situation is presented in Figure 2.



Fig. 1

Fig. 2



Snapping is enabled.




Advanced Digitizing panel from View, Panels menu is visible.




Left click on the plugin icon to start.



Click Enable advanced digitizing tool button.



Move the cursor to snap point A and left click.




Press c to enter construction mode



Snap the endpoint of a segment of length 68,000 and left click.



Press c to disable construction mode


Move the cursor and snap the other end of the segment. Right click.



Move the cursor to snap point B and left click.


Press c to enter construction mode


Snap the endpoint of a segment of length 37.000 and left click.



Press c to disable construction mode


Move the cursor and snap the other end of the segment.



Right click.



Move the cursor above points A and B. Left click.



Now you should check the obtained distances, because calculations are performed on floating point numbers with some precision. Click the Measure Line button.



Click Cartesian.




Click on points A, C and B in turn.


The measured distances in this example are: 68.000000±0.000001 and 37.000000±0.000001.
